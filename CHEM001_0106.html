1. The SI System

Scientists use the International System of Units (SI) to ensure consistency in measurements worldwide.

Key Idea: The SI system standardizes measurements for global scientific communication.
Quantity SI Unit Symbol
Length meter m
Mass kilogram kg
Time second s
Temperature kelvin K

Quick Check

3. Dimensional Analysis

Dimensional analysis is a method used to convert units by multiplying by conversion factors.

Key Idea: Units cancel out just like numbers, leaving the desired unit.

Practice converting units using dimensional analysis.

Example:
Convert 100 cm to meters:

100 cm × (1 m / 100 cm) = 1 m

Challenge Problem

Convert 2500 g to kilograms using dimensional analysis.

2500 g × (1 kg / 1000 g) = 2.5 kg
